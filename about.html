Company Overview

We are different and we proudly embrace it.

Witr Supply and logistic services Company is a privately owned company that provides logistics, transportation, clearing, and forwarding solutions. Headquartered in Mogadishu, Somalia, we have substantial operating capacity, powered by a team of highly professional staff with experience in the field.

We believe in setting transparent benchmarks, providing incredibly robust and highly efficient transport pipelines. We go beyond simple tracking; we engineer reliability.

  • Over 15 years of unmatched logistics excellence
  • Absolute safety and rigorous regulatory compliance
  • Professional teams driving a global mindset
